Some projects

Evaluation of Key Performance Indicadors of an Airport with the analysis of aircraft trajectories

In this project I analized some performance variables of an airport such as Airport Throughput, Runway Occupancy Time and Taxi time to discuss with relevant stakeholders of the airport and define the steps to take to increase the airport capacity and efficiency in operation. The project was mainly done in Matlab and excel, and consisted in analyzing ADS-B trayectories. ADS-B is a technology to in which aircraft broadcast some flight variables such as position, speed, etc, together with the time stamp of the data.

Traffic analysis of an airport

In this project, I analized the air traffic of an airport including number of Air Traffic Movements (ATMs) during one day and the aircraft mix operating in the airport.

Debugging of an airport operational database

In this project, used Python and excel to help an airport to debug their operational database by querying external databases and using web scrapping.
